    => Minimal correction needed (alpha* ~ 0.02-0.04, near unadjusted)
    => VWN-Lin retains country effects that BH's step-up rule discards

  Correction cuts both ways: it can remove marginally-significant results
  or restore ones that FDR procedures discard. See results_note.pdf.
