# mhtopt 1.0.0

* First public release.
* Functions: `mht_critical`, `mht_test`, `mht_est`, `mht_cost_estimate`, `mht_table`.
* Two cost models: Linear (FDA calibration) and Cobb-Douglas (J-PAL calibration).
* Reports optimal, Bonferroni, Holm, BH, and unadjusted decisions side by side.
* Vignette: "Optimal Multiple Hypothesis Testing".
